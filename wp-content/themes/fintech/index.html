How Cygnature works?

Follow this simple process to kick start your e-signature journey with Cygnature

How to utilize Cygnature as an eSignature solution?

Cygnature is a commercially viable, smooth, and hassle-free eSignature solution. Our electronic signature software, Cygnature, follows a common basic procedure. The following helps you understand the procedure in a better way.

Creating an account

Register with Cygnature and create your account with 2-Factor Authentication

Uploading the relevant document

The document that needs to be e-signed is uploaded from the folder, google drive, one drive, drop box

Flow of document creation in Cygnature

This deals with organizing an uploaded document and making it ready for you to sign the document digitally. The steps included in this workflow include:

Sign up for a Cygnature account
Uploading a document for signing
Selecting Signers and Observers for the signing process
Choosing to sign the document in parallel or sequentially
Sending the document to signers
Selecting the types of signatures and placeholder for signing 

Flow of document signing

The steps are as follows: 

A signer creates a Cygnature account
Accept the invitation via user to sign a particular document
Select the Signature Type to sign the document
Sign the document on the placeholder given
Contact Us

Blockchain enabled signing process

All details regarding the paper trail of the online document are recorded here. This workflow includes the following processes: 

The system records the signing process
It tracks and creates reminders for the signers. 
It LIVE tracks the important details about the signers like Device ID, IP address, Browser used, Geo-tags, Latitude & longitude, Timestamp.
The system creates a certificate of completion which is hashed in Block Chain. 

Why Cygnature?

Enhanced security and legality

The incorporation of security controls in the verification process protects your documents and keeps them safe. The fact that most countries have legalized the e-signature in some form makes it a fast and convenient way to authenticate documents.

Detailed document tracking

Having a detailed audit trail including the time of signing and the people involved makes it easy for you to track hold-ups.

Convenience and mobility

Your signature can be affixed to the required document from anywhere anytime. This enhances its efficiency and makes it convenient for you to sign documents on the go. The mobility of our e-signing solution, Cygnature, is another advantage that has resulted in its wide acceptance. 

Cost-effective and quick

When you need to get signature of someone on a document in a small timespan, e-signing can be a quick method. Gaining signatures from different signatories on multiple documents without the use of paper makes it cost-effective.
